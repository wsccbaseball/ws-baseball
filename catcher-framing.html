Metric Definitions
Called pitches only — StrikeCalled & BallCalled. Swings/fouls/in-play excluded.
Shadow zone — 1.455 inches (half baseball diameter) outside zone edge, 3 rings.
Weights — Ring 1 (inner) ×1.0 · Ring 2 ×1.4 · Ring 3 (outer) ×1.8.
Stolen Strikes — StrikeCalled outside zone. Lost Strikes — BallCalled inside zone.
Framing Runs (RE24) — count & base/out adjusted run value of framing.
Framing Score — weighted shadow strike% vs 63% avg. >105 = above average.
